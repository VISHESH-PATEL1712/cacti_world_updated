Introduction:


Although the Euphorbia horrida has been referred to as a "cactus," it's not a real cactus. It is a succulent plant which is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.


Native Habitat:
The arid parts of southern Africa, including states like South Africa and Namibia, are host to the euphorbia horrida tree.

How often to water cowboy cactus?


Euphorbia should be watered every two weeks, with a full two-week dry period in between.


Allow the Soil to Dry: Water the cowboy cactus when the top inch (2.5 cm) of the soil feels dry to the touch. Insert your finger into the soil to gauge its moisture level. Avoid watering if the soil still feels damp.


Adjust with the Seasons: During the active growing season (spring and summer), you'll likely need to water more frequently.
